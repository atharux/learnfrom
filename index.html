The Problem

You've seen this before.

Six months of work. A launch. Then silence. The dashboard gets one look and never opens again. The internal tool everyone asked for sits unused. The redesigned process creates new complaints instead of solving old ones.

It's not a resources problem or a talent problem. It's a method problem. Nobody ran the research. Nobody mapped the journey. Nobody defined what "better" looked like before building — or measured whether it got there.

This course gives you the method.
